SDG 2: Zero Hunger
AVONIC supports sustainable agriculture
by improving compost quality and plant
health, helping local farmers grow more
nutritious crops with fewer resources.
SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production
By recycling organic waste through vermicomposting,
AVONIC promotes responsible production and consumption, reducing br landfill waste and closing the nutrient loop in farming.
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

AVONIC is a localized vermicomposting system that automates environmental control using sensors and an ESP32-powered dashboard for small-scale farms.

It monitors soil moisture, gas, and temperature, and automatically adjusts humidity while displaying live data on a web dashboard.
